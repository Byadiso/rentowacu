All properties in one place

We are a search engine for houses for rent. In addition to the many landlords who post their ads on our platform, we search the web for all available properties and collect them in one place.

Compare and save money

Filter your search by city, rent, house type, etc. to see a list of homes that match exactly your search criteria. You can also create a search agent that will send you emails when a new property that meets your expectations appears on our site.

Fast contact

After finding your dream rental offer, you will be able to contact the owner to arrange a visit, and then receive the keys to your new home. If you need help, please contact our customer service.

Apartments and houses for rent

Are you looking for a new property to rent? Then you've come to the right place. At Rentola, we facilitate contact between tenants and landlords, making it easy to find each other. Whether you want to rent your new apartment, house or room, you will find a large selection in all price ranges. We have rooms and flats for rent all over the country, same goes for vacant houses. Use the search box at the top of the page, enter the area you want to live in, then tap the Search button. Then you will be presented with a ton of options that you can read more about and see pictures. You can also use our smart filtering options to find your new dream home.
